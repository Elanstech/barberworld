Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Barber World, our products, and services.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times depend on your selected method:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 3-4 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day

Processing time is 1-2 business days before shipment. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer FREE standard shipping on all orders over $99 within the continental United States. For orders under $99, standard shipping rates apply.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to all 50 U.S. states and Canada. International shipping rates vary by destination. Additional customs fees and import duties may apply and are the customer's responsibility.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled or edited before they are packed for fulfillment. You can use the order editing link in your confirmation email or contact us immediately at barberworldnyc@gmail.com.

Important: Cancellations are issued as store credit only. Once an order is packed, it cannot be canceled.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and carrier information. You can use this to track your package directly on the carrier's website (USPS, UPS, or FedEx).

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

ALL SALES ARE FINAL. Due to high order volumes, we cannot accept returns or exchanges.

However, we handle each case individually. We may replace or refund products if:

  • They arrive with physical damage or defects
  • You receive incorrect items

You must notify us within 2 days of receipt and provide clear photos. See our full Return Policy for details.

How do I get a refund for damaged items?

Contact us at barberworldnyc@gmail.com within 2 days of receiving your order with:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos of the damage
  • Description of the issue

We'll review your case and provide instructions for a replacement or refund.

What if I receive the wrong item?

Contact us immediately with photos of the incorrect item. We'll provide a free return label and send the correct item or issue a refund once we receive the return.

My package was lost or stolen - what do I do?

If you purchased Route Protection at checkout, file a claim directly at Route Protection Claims with your order number and email.

Without Route Protection, your order may be covered up to $200 by the shipping carrier. Contact us and we'll file a claim for you.

Payment & Security

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover

  • Klarna (Buy Now, Pay Later)

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use Stripe for payment processing, which employs industry-standard encryption and security measures. We never store your full credit card information on our servers. All transactions are PCI DSS compliant.

Do you charge sales tax?

Yes, sales tax is calculated based on your shipping address and local tax rates. The exact amount will be displayed during checkout before you complete your purchase.
