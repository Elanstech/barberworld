Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Barber World, our products, and services.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times depend on your selected method:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 3-4 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day

Processing time is 1-2 business days before shipment. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer FREE standard shipping on all orders over $75 within the continental United States. For orders under $75, standard shipping is $8.95.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to all 50 U.S. states and Canada. International shipping rates vary by destination. Additional customs fees and import duties may apply and are the customer's responsibility.

Can I change or cancel my order?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, we can usually modify or cancel it. Contact us immediately at barberworldnyc@gmail.com with your order number. Once shipped, standard return policies apply.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and carrier information. You can use this to track your package directly on the carrier's website (USPS, UPS, or FedEx).

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Items must be:

  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all accessories
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase

Contact us for a return authorization before shipping items back. See our full Return Policy for details.

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Inspection: 1-2 business days
  • Processing: 3-5 business days
  • Bank processing: 5-10 business days

Total time is typically 10-15 business days from when we receive your return.

Who pays for return shipping?

Return shipping is typically the customer's responsibility. However, we provide free return shipping if:

  • The item is defective or damaged
  • We sent the wrong item
  • There was an error on our part

Payment & Security

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Klarna (Buy Now, Pay Later)
  • Afterpay

All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use Stripe for payment processing, which employs industry-standard encryption and security measures. We never store your full credit card information on our servers. All transactions are PCI DSS compliant.

Do you charge sales tax?

Yes, sales tax is calculated based on your shipping address and local tax rates. The exact amount will be displayed during checkout before you complete your purchase.

Account & Website

Do I need an account to place an order?

No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to:

  • Track orders easily
  • Save addresses for faster checkout
  • View order history
  • Receive exclusive offers
How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Account" or "Login" button, then select "Forgot Password." Enter your email address and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Can I modify my account information?

Yes! Log into your account and navigate to account settings. You can update your email, password, addresses, and communication preferences at any time.
