Return Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

ALL SALES ARE FINAL

Due to high order volumes, we are unable to accept returns or exchanges. All unauthorized returns will incur a 45% restocking fee.

Our Return Policy

ALL SALES ARE FINAL. Barber World does not accept returns or exchanges on products purchased from our site.

However, we handle each case individually. We may replace or refund products if they arrive damaged, defective, or if you receive incorrect items.

Exceptions to Our Policy

We may accept returns and provide replacements or refunds in the following cases:

  • Physical damage: Container damage, bottle defects, or defects in the label or product appearance
  • Incorrect items: You received the wrong product

How to Request a Return

To qualify for a replacement or refund, you must:

  1. Contact us within 2 days of receiving your shipment
  2. Provide clear photos of the damaged, defective, or incorrect items
  3. Wait for authorization: Do not ship items back without our approval

For incorrect items, we will provide a free return label via email. Once the unused, incorrect items are returned to us, we will send out the correct items or issue a refund. Refunds will only be issued once the returned items are received.

For help processing a return, email us at barberworldnyc@gmail.com

Unauthorized Returns

Important: Returns shipped without prior authorization will incur a 45% restocking fee. Always contact us before returning any items.

Damaged or Defective Items

  • Shipping damage: May be covered under shipping insurance. Contact us at barberworldnyc@gmail.com for assistance.
  • Manufacturing defects: Require a claim with the manufacturer under warranty. See our Warranty Page for details.
  • Barber World-branded items: Contact us directly and we'll assist with the return or replacement process.

Order Cancellations

Effective January 15, 2025:

  • Orders can be canceled before packing for fulfillment - store credit only
  • Cancel or edit your order using the link in your confirmation email
  • Once packed, orders cannot be canceled
  • Preorder cancellations receive store credit only

Lost, Damaged, or Stolen Orders

  • Route Protection purchased: File a claim at Route Protection Claims with your order number and email
  • No Route Protection: Coverage up to $200 may be available from the shipping carrier. Contact us and we'll file a claim for you.

Contact Us

Have questions about returns or need assistance?

Email: barberworldnyc@gmail.com

Response Time: We typically respond within 24 hours (Monday-Friday)

Barber World reserves the right to refuse returns that do not meet the eligibility criteria. This policy is subject to change without notice. By making a purchase, you agree to these terms.
