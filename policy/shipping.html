Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Processing Time

At Barber World, we strive to process and ship your order as quickly as possible:

  • Standard Processing: 1-2 business days
  • Peak Season: 2-3 business days (holidays and promotional periods)
  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day
  • You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships

Shipping Methods & Rates

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

FREE STANDARD SHIPPING

On all orders over $75 within the continental United States!

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): $8.95
  • Expedited Shipping (3-4 business days): $15.95
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days): $25.95
  • Overnight Shipping (1 business day): $39.95

Shipping Locations

Domestic Shipping: We ship to all 50 United States, including Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. territories.

International Shipping: Currently, we ship to Canada. International shipping rates and times vary based on destination. Additional customs fees and import duties may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive:

  • Email confirmation with tracking number
  • Carrier information (USPS, UPS, or FedEx)
  • Direct link to track your package
  • Estimated delivery date

Pro Tip: Please allow 24 hours for tracking information to update in the carrier's system.

Packaging

All products are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition:

  • Professional-grade protective packaging materials
  • Secure boxing to prevent damage during transit
  • Discreet packaging with no external branding (upon request)
  • All manufacturer packaging and documentation included

Delivery Issues

If you experience any issues with delivery:

  • Lost Packages: Contact us if tracking shows no movement for 10+ days
  • Damaged Packages: Please photograph the damage and contact us within 48 hours
  • Incorrect Address: Contact us immediately if you notice an error in your shipping address
  • Refused Delivery: Additional fees may apply for refused packages

Need Help? Contact us at barberworldnyc@gmail.com for assistance with any shipping issues.

Holiday Shipping

During holiday seasons, please note:

  • Processing times may be extended by 1-2 business days
  • Carrier delays are possible during peak shipping periods
  • We recommend ordering early to ensure timely delivery
  • Holiday cutoff dates will be posted on our website

Insurance & Signature

For your protection:

  • All orders over $300 are automatically insured and require signature upon delivery
  • Additional insurance can be purchased for orders under $300
  • Signature confirmation can be added to any order for $5
  • We are not responsible for packages marked as "delivered" by the carrier
