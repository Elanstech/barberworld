Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

FREE SHIPPING

On all orders over $99 within the continental United States!

Shipping Rates

We offer competitive shipping rates through our trusted shipping partners:

  • Orders over $99: FREE SHIPPING
  • Orders under $99: $8.95 flat rate shipping

Shipping Protection Available: Add shipping protection for just $5 at checkout to safeguard your order against loss, theft, or damage during transit.

Shipping Carriers

All orders are shipped through UPS for reliable and fast delivery.

If you are located in a remote area, your order may be shipped through an alternative carrier to ensure timely delivery.

Processing Time

At Barber World, we strive to process and ship your order as quickly as possible:

  • Standard Processing: 1-2 business days
  • Peak Season: 2-3 business days (holidays and promotional periods)
  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a personalized tracking email from Shippo, our shipping platform.

Your tracking email will include:

  • Your unique tracking number
  • Carrier information (UPS or alternative)
  • Direct link to track your package
  • Estimated delivery date

Pro Tip: Please allow 24 hours for tracking information to update in the carrier's system after receiving your tracking email.

Shipping Protection

We strongly recommend adding shipping protection at checkout for just $5.

Shipping protection covers:

  • Lost packages
  • Stolen packages
  • Damaged items during transit
  • Missing items

If you purchase shipping protection and experience any of these issues, you can file a claim for a replacement or refund.

Without shipping protection: Your order may be covered up to $200 by the shipping carrier. However, the claims process can take longer and approval is not guaranteed.

Estimated Delivery Times

Delivery times vary based on your location:

  • Standard Delivery: 3-7 business days after shipment
  • Remote Areas: May take 5-10 business days

Total time from order placement to delivery is typically 4-9 business days, including processing time.

Shipping Locations

Domestic Shipping: We ship to all 50 United States, including Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. territories.

International Shipping: Currently, we ship to Canada. International shipping rates and times vary based on destination. Additional customs fees and import duties may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

Delivery Issues

If you experience any issues with delivery:

  • Lost Packages: Contact us if tracking shows no movement for 10+ days
  • Damaged Packages: Photograph the damage and contact us within 2 days of receipt
  • Stolen Packages: If marked as delivered but not received, file a claim with shipping protection or contact us immediately
  • Incorrect Address: Contact us immediately if you notice an error in your shipping address before shipment

Need Help? Contact us at barberworldnyc@gmail.com for assistance with any shipping issues.

Packaging

All products are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition:

  • Professional-grade protective packaging materials
  • Secure boxing to prevent damage during transit
  • Discreet packaging available upon request
  • All manufacturer packaging and documentation included

Holiday Shipping

During holiday seasons, please note:

  • Processing times may be extended by 1-2 business days
  • Carrier delays are possible during peak shipping periods
  • We recommend ordering early to ensure timely delivery
  • Holiday cutoff dates will be posted on our website

Questions?
