What Warranties Cover

Manufacturer warranties typically cover:

  • Defects in materials and workmanship
  • Manufacturing defects
  • Motor and electrical component failures
  • Blade defects (if not from wear)
  • Housing and structural issues

What Warranties Don't Cover

Important: Warranties do not cover:

  • Normal wear and tear from regular use
  • Damage from misuse or abuse
  • Accidental damage or drops
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Use of non-original parts or accessories
  • Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect function
  • Blade sharpening or replacement
  • Battery degradation over time
  • Damage from improper cleaning or maintenance

How to File a Warranty Claim

Follow these steps for warranty service:

  1. Verify Warranty Period: Check your purchase date and warranty documentation
  2. Contact Us First: Email barberworldnyc@gmail.com with your issue
    • Include order number and purchase date
    • Describe the problem in detail
    • Include photos or videos if applicable
  3. We'll Guide You: We'll help determine if it's a warranty issue and provide manufacturer contact information
  4. Contact Manufacturer: Reach out to the manufacturer's warranty department with:
    • Proof of purchase (we'll provide this)
    • Product serial number
    • Description of the problem
  5. Follow Manufacturer Instructions: They'll provide return shipping labels or repair instructions

Keeping Warranty Documentation

To ensure smooth warranty claims:

  • Keep your order confirmation email
  • Retain manufacturer warranty cards
  • Save product manuals and documentation
  • Note your product serial numbers
  • Keep original packaging when possible

Pro Tip: Take photos of serial numbers and register products with manufacturers immediately after purchase for faster warranty service.

Warranty Claim Timeline

Typical warranty claim process:

  • Day 1: Contact us or manufacturer
  • Days 2-3: Evaluation and approval
  • Days 4-7: Shipping to repair facility
  • Days 8-21: Repair or replacement processing
  • Days 22-28: Return shipping

Total time varies by manufacturer and issue complexity. We'll help expedite when possible.

Warranty Repairs vs. Replacement

Manufacturers may choose to:

  • Repair: Fix the defective product and return it
  • Replace: Send a new or refurbished unit
  • Refund: Provide credit or refund (rare)

The decision is at the manufacturer's discretion based on the issue and product availability.

Warranty FAQs

Q: What if the manufacturer denies my warranty claim?

Q: Do I need to register my product?

Q: Can I still get warranty service without my receipt?
