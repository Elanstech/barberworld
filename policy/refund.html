Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Customer Satisfaction Guaranteed: We stand behind the quality of our products and are committed to ensuring your complete satisfaction.

Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • Item is returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Product is unused and in original condition
  • All original packaging and accessories are included
  • Product has not been damaged by customer
  • Return is approved by our customer service team

Refund Amounts

Your refund will include:

  • Product Cost: Full purchase price of the item
  • Taxes: Any applicable taxes paid

Your refund will NOT include:

  • Original Shipping Costs: Shipping charges are non-refundable
  • Return Shipping: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • Restocking Fees: May apply in certain situations (see below)

Refund Processing Time

Once we receive and inspect your return:

Timeline Overview:

  • Day 1-2: Return received and inspected
  • Day 3-5: Refund approved and processed
  • Day 5-10: Refund appears in your account
  • You'll receive an email confirmation when we receive your return
  • Inspection typically takes 1-2 business days
  • Once approved, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days
  • Bank processing may take an additional 5-10 business days

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refund will appear on your statement within 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: Refund will appear in your PayPal account within 24-48 hours
  • Apple Pay: Refund will appear on the card used through Apple Pay
  • Klarna/Afterpay: Refund processed according to their policies

We cannot issue refunds to a different payment method or card than originally used.

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following situations:

  • Item shows obvious signs of use
  • Item is missing parts or accessories
  • Item is returned more than 30 days after delivery
  • Item packaging is damaged or missing
  • Item is not in original condition due to customer fault

The amount deducted will depend on the condition of the returned item.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance"
  • Opened hygiene products (for safety reasons)
  • Used or damaged items (unless defective)
  • Items purchased more than 30 days ago
  • Gift cards or promotional items
  • Customized or personalized products

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item:

  • Full refund including original shipping costs
  • Free return shipping label provided
  • Priority processing (1-2 business days)
  • Option to receive replacement instead of refund
  • Must report within 48 hours of delivery

Please Note: Photos of the defect or damage must be provided to expedite your claim.

Wrong Item Received

If we ship you the wrong item:

  • Full refund including all shipping costs
  • Free return shipping label provided
  • Express replacement shipped at no charge
  • Priority processing with our sincerest apologies

Restocking Fees

Barber World does not typically charge restocking fees. However, fees may apply if:

  • 15% Fee: Return without authorization number
  • 20% Fee: Item missing original packaging
  • 25% Fee: Item shows signs of use or damage
  • No Refund: Item significantly damaged or incomplete

To avoid restocking fees, always contact us for a return authorization before shipping items back.

Cancelled Orders

If you need to cancel your order:

  • Before Shipment: Full refund (typically within 3-5 business days)
  • After Shipment: Standard return policy applies once delivered
  • In Transit: Wait for delivery, then initiate return

Contact us immediately at barberworldnyc@gmail.com to request cancellation.

Refund Delays

If you haven't received your refund within the expected timeframe:

  • Check your bank or credit card statement again
  • Contact your bank or credit card company (processing times vary)
  • Verify the refund was sent to the correct account
  • If you've checked all of these and still don't see your refund, email us at barberworldnyc@gmail.com with your order number

Exchanges vs. Refunds

Prefer an exchange instead? We offer:

  • Same product in different color or size
  • Different product of equal or greater value
  • Faster processing than refund + new order
  • Free exchange shipping on defective items

Contact us to discuss exchange options before returning your item.

Price Adjustments

If an item goes on sale within 7 days of your purchase:

  • Contact us for a price adjustment
  • We'll refund the difference
  • Must be the exact same item
  • Clearance and final sale items excluded
  • Only one price adjustment per item

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Email barberworldnyc@gmail.com with your order number
  2. Include reason for return and any photos (if applicable)
  3. Wait for return authorization and instructions
  4. Pack item securely in original packaging
  5. Ship with tracking number
  6. Receive confirmation and refund within 5-10 business days

Questions About Your Refund?

We're here to help!

Email: barberworldnyc@gmail.com

Subject: Include "Refund Request" and your order number

Response Time: Within 24 hours (Monday-Friday)
